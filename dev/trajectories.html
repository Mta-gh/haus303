Trajectories

2019 - 120 pages

Choosing as subjects many brutalist buildings in London that are either loved or hated (but that have become cult for architecture enthusiasts in any case), as well more recent constructions; Trajectories explores the recurrent idea, in our work as Haus303, of the journey to "meet" architecture. This book compares and highlights the subtile similarities and differences in the perception of the same buildings but by two different photographers.
