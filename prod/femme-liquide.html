La Femme Liquide

Nadia Arab

2021

La Femme liquide is part of a larger research on an idea of the body victim of liquid* marginalization. In other terms, research that draws the contours of bodies set aside because of their organic or symbolic liquidity(...). We can suppose that a liquid body is a body out of control, of without control, as opposed to solid, that is frozen, that restrains and controls itself. In contrary to humankind, nature doesn't seem to be afraid of liquidity, which is a state like another, a force of life, of sharing and fusion.
